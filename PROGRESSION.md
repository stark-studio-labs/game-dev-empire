# Tech Empire — Gated Progression (Single Source of Truth)

Every unlock in the game is defined here. Nothing should be available before its gate.

---

## Eras

| Era | Years | Office | Unlocks |
|-----|-------|--------|---------|
| **Garage** | Y1-5 | Garage (1 person) | Small games, 30 starter topics, basic genres |
| **Startup** | Y5-12 | Small Office (5 staff) | Medium games, training, marketing, 20 more topics |
| **Scale-Up** | Y12-20 | Medium Office (5 staff) | Large games, R&D Lab, research tree, 30 more topics |
| **Empire** | Y20+ | Large Office (8+ staff) | AAA games, hardware lab, verticals, all remaining topics |

## Genre Unlocks

All 6 genres available from start (Action, Adventure, RPG, Simulation, Strategy, Casual).

---

## Platform Unlocks

Platforms unlock based on the in-game timeline (they "launch" at their published date and "retire" at their retire date). Not gated by player progress — gated by game year.

---

## Dev Mode

Toggle in Settings → Dev Mode ON:
- All features unlocked
- All topics available
- All platforms available
- Unlimited cash cheat
- Speed 8x available
- Skip tutorial

For internal testing only. Hidden from regular players (requires 5-click easter egg on version number to show toggle).
